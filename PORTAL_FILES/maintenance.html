About WAROFGALAXYZ

We invite you to go to an independent, unique and mysterious universe in which chaos coexists with ancient orders.

The #WOG universe contains one grandiose in scope and complexity galaxy, divided into 200 independent solar systems, in each of which the player can find 20 planets with unique characteristics and inhabitants.

The choice of the gamer has two options for the development of the plot: Combat and Peace. Regardless of the chosen scenarios for the development of the plot, you can independently model a unique race whose fighters will have unique skills.

Of course, the seizure of solar empires is impossible without excellent combat equipment: for this game #WOG offers gamers a wide selection of weapons and equipment, some of which can be bought for donations or received as a reward for the victory over the hostile civilization.
